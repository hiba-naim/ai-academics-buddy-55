# 🎓 AI Academic Copilot

**AI Academic Copilot** is an AI-powered academic management platform that helps university students organize their academic life, track their performance, and make smarter decisions throughout the semester.

Instead of relying on multiple disconnected tools, AI Academic Copilot centralizes course management, task planning, GPA tracking, registration planning, instructor reviews, and academic analytics into one intelligent workspace.

---

## 🚀 Motivation

University students often struggle with:

- Keeping track of assignments and deadlines
- Monitoring GPA progress
- Planning future semesters
- Choosing instructors
- Understanding their academic performance

These tasks are usually spread across multiple applications, making it difficult to stay organized and make informed decisions.

AI Academic Copilot was built to solve this problem by providing a single platform enhanced with AI-powered insights and personalized recommendations.

- Academic overview

- Organize courses by semester

AI Academic Copilot analyzes student academic data to provide personalized guidance such as:
